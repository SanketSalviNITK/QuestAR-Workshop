# Session 4: The Creator's Lab 🎨

It's time to take what you've learned and build something unique! You can work in groups or solo.

## 🌈 Project Ideas
1. **AR Business Card**: Scan a logo and show your portfolio links in 3D.
2. **AR Art Gallery**: Make 3D sculptures appear on printed photos.
3. **AR Education**: Scan a map and show 3D facts about a country.
4. **AR Mini-Game**: Create a scavenger hunt with multiple markers.

## 🛠️ How to Remix
1. **Change the 3D Model**: Replace the `OctahedronGeometry` with `BoxGeometry`, `SphereGeometry`, or `TorusKnotGeometry`.
2. **Change the Color**: Modify the `color` hex code in the `MeshStandardMaterial`.
3. **Change the Marker**: 
   - Visit the [Mind.AR Compiler](https://hiukim.github.io/mind-ar-js-doc/tools/compile/).
   - Upload your own image (logo, photo, card).
   - Download the `.mind` file and update `imageTargetSrc` in your code.

## 🚀 Final Presentation
Once you've built your idea:
1. `git add .`, `git commit -m "Remix: My AR Idea"`, `git push`.
2. Wait for Vercel to deploy.
3. Share your URL with the class!
